Jeff Bezos , byname of Jeffrey Preston Bezos,(born January 12, 1964,New Mexico, U.S.),

American entrepreneur who played a key role in the growth of e-commerce as the founder

and chief executive officer of Amazon.com, Inc., an online merchant of books and later

of a wide variety of products.Under his guidance, Amazon became the largest retailer on

the World Wide Web and the model for Internet sales.
